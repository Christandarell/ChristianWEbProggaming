Plot Overview

Despicable Me is an animated comedy film that follows the story of Gru, a supervillain who plans to steal the moon. However, his life takes an unexpected turn when he adopts three orphan girls as part of a scheme to steal a shrink ray from his rival, Vector. As Gru struggles with his new role as a father, he begins to question his life of crime and discovers the true meaning of family.

Cast
